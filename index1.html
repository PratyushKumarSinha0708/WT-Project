Course We Offer

We offer the best engineering courses at our University

BE-CSE

BE Computer Science Engineering (CSE) is a 4-year undergraduate program focusing on computer hardware and software. It equips you with skills to design, develop, and maintain computer systems and software applications.

BE-IT

BE Information Technology (IT) is a 4-year undergraduate program focusing on IT infrastructure, networking, and software applications. It prepares you for roles in IT support, network administration, and software development.

BE-ECE

BE Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) is a 4-year undergraduate program focusing on electronic circuits, communication systems, and signal processing. It prepares you for roles in electronics design, telecommunications, and embedded systems.

BE-MECH

BE Mechanical Engineering is a 4-year undergraduate program focusing on the design, analysis, and manufacturing of mechanical systems. It prepares you for roles in automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing industries.
